At SJSU’s AR/VR Club, I managed all of our social media platforms, ensuring that content was posted consistently, visually engaging, and reflective of the club’s innovative spirit. I designed posts, flyers, logos, and illustrations to promote events, helping strengthen the club’s visual identity and increase student outreach. One of my favorite parts was creating the club’s official mascot, a purple monkey that became a central part of our branding. Through creative design and thoughtful storytelling, I helped increase the club’s presence on campus and build a more recognizable identity.
