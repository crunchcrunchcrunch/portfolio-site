During my time supporting Stanford Hospital’s cafeteria department, I redesigned menu layouts, food labels, and marketing materials to create a clearer and more engaging experience for patients and staff. I also designed posters for events catered to Stanford’s healthcare workers, helping promote community. My main role was supporting sustainability initiatives, promoting monthly cafeteria specials, and highlighting local farms and organic food companies that partner with Stanford. Through thoughtful design and communication, I helped elevate the cafeteria’s brand and supported its mission of promoting sustainable, healthy food.
