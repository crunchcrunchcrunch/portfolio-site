LIMO is a music venue that supports San Jose’s local music scene, from indie and hardcore to EDM. I designed physical and digital promotional materials, ranging from flyers and business cards to social media graphics to promote their shows. Working with such a diverse range of music genres, I adapted each design to fit the unique energy of every artist and event. I also created motion graphics and animated posters that were posted to social media to grab people’s attention.
